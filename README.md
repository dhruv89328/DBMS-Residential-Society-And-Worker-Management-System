# 🏢 Residential Society & Work Management System

A comprehensive **Database Management System (DBMS)** project that streamlines residential society operations by managing residents, flats, maintenance, complaints, visitors, security, parking, amenities, and work allocation through a centralized relational database.

---

## 🚀 Overview

Residential societies often rely on manual processes to manage residents, maintenance, complaints, and service requests. This project digitizes these operations using a normalized relational database, improving efficiency, transparency, and data integrity.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 👥 Resident Management
- Maintain resident profiles
- Store contact details and email information
- Track owners and tenants

### 🏢 Building & Flat Management
- Manage multiple buildings
- Allocate flats to residents
- Track occupancy and ownership

### 💰 Maintenance Management
- Record monthly maintenance charges
- Track payment history
- Support multiple payment methods

### 🛠 Complaint Management
- Register maintenance complaints
- Track complaint status
- Maintain complaint history

### 🚪 Visitor Management
- Record visitor entry and exit
- Associate visitors with residents
- Improve society security

### 🚗 Vehicle & Parking Management
- Register resident vehicles
- Allocate parking slots
- Prevent duplicate assignments

### 🏸 Amenity Booking
- Manage society amenities
- Book facilities
- Track booking schedules

### 🏠 Rental Management
- Maintain tenant records

### 👮 Security Management
- Manage security guard information

### 📢 Notice Board
- Publish society notices

# ⭐ Unique Selling Point (USP)

## 🔧 Smart Job Allocation System

Unlike traditional society management systems, this project introduces a **Job Allocation Module**.

Whenever a resident raises a complaint:

- The complaint is converted into a work order.
- A suitable worker is assigned based on the job type.
- Worker availability is considered.
- Job progress is tracked from assignment to completion.
- Estimated work cost is recorded.
- The administrator can monitor every assigned task.

This minimizes manual effort, improves accountability, and ensures faster complaint resolution.
